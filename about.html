About Me

My name is Benedict Wambua, known artistically as Lulu B. I was born on May 10, 2003, and I am from the Kamba tribe in Machakos County, Kenya.

I am a love song artist, a web developer, and a coding enthusiast. My music expresses emotions and stories of love, while my coding journey empowers me to build digital solutions.

I studied at AIC Primary School Kithimani and Kithimani HGM Secondary School. Currently, I’m pursuing a Diploma in Computer Programming at Borabu Technical Training Institute.

Beyond music and coding, I’ve gained skills in Microsoft Office, networking, and hospital system management during my attachment at Masinga Level 4 Hospital.
